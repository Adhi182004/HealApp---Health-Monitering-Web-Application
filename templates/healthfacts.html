Quick Health Facts You Should Know
Bananas can boost mood due to their high levels of tryptophan, which converts into serotonin.
Drinking enough water daily improves brain function and energy levels.
Regular walking reduces the risk of heart disease and helps burn calories.
Laughter boosts the immune system and reduces stress hormones.
A lack of sleep increases the risk of obesity and diabetes.
Dark chocolate (in moderation) can improve brain function and heart health.
Leafy greens like spinach are rich in iron and improve blood circulation.
Deep breathing exercises can help reduce anxiety and stress naturally.
Dopamine plays a key role in motivation and reward. Listening to music or achieving small goals can boost dopamine levels.
Serotonin helps regulate mood, sleep, and digestion. Sunlight exposure and exercise naturally boost serotonin levels.
